Welcome to TimeSense


TimeSense shows you where your time is going.

It visualizes your life according to your Google Calendar
and gives you the data you need to make informed decisions
about what you spend your time doing








All calendar data is stored locally in your browser. No calendar data is ever sent to TimeSense.
Authentication is done with Google directly via the industry-standard OAuth so TimeSense never sees your password.
